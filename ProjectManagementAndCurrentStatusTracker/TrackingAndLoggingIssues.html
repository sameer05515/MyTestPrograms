  • Where I can track the issues I am facing while development at home.
  • Sometimes the issues may not be solved in a day, then in that tool I can schedule (or at least mark the current status) so that I may further solve the issue

Steps to new requirement processing
Customer logs one requirement
defect/requirement repository
on the basis of logged requirement tickets are raised to develop
develper takes ticket and develop
New requirement is added in new release
Steps to defect processing
Customer logs one defect
defect/requirement repository
On the basis of loged defect ticket raised to solve it and the defect is fixed in next coming release.
Developer fixed the bug
Bug fix goes in new release

Roles

Admin who
  1. assigns ticket in right queue/category, to right person or group
  2. creates new category
  3. adds new person in a group
Developer who
  1. develops the new requirement
  2. does the bug fixes
  3. Solves the assigned tickets
User who
  1. logs ticket
  2. demands for new requirement
  3. demands for bug fixes
  4. Confirms and closes his ticket
  5. Can re-open in case not satisfied with the new releases
